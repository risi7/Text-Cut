ABOUT



Text-Cut is a text summarizing web-app. It is build using ML-NLP (Machine Learning - Natural Language Processing) Techniques. It comprises of both Extractive and Abstractive Text Summarization methods.


Extractive Based Method :-

> A subset of words that represent the most important points are pulled from vast text and combined.

> Think it of as a text HILIGHTED in book showing important info.

> Methodologies :- TextRank, LexRank, TfIdf, KLSum, etc..


Abstractive Based Method :-

> Advanced deep learning techniques are applied to paraphrase and shorten text.

> Think it of as reading and writing important info from original doc with a pen.

> Methodologies :- GPT, T5, BERT, etc..
